# Major Arcana card art — provenance & license

Source: **Rider-Waite-Smith tarot deck, 1909 "Roses & Lilies" edition** (Rider Company, London).
Illustrations by **Pamela Colman Smith** (1878-1951), under direction of A. E. Waite.

- The original 1909 deck is **public domain** in the United States (published before 1930)
  and in the United Kingdom.
- Files were downloaded from Wikimedia Commons scans of the original 1909 printing:
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Rider-Waite_tarot_deck
  (per-file: `https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:RWS_Tarot_<NN>_<Name>.jpg`)
- Processing applied in this repo: downscale to height 512, JPEG re-encode (quality 87),
  metadata stripped. No other alterations.
- Copyright caveat recorded for future contributors: **modern recolored editions of this deck
  (e.g. US Games versions) are NOT public domain.** Only scans of the original 1909 printing
  may be added here.
